About the Game

Bounce Mania is a thrilling arcade platformer that puts your reflexes to the test. Guide a bouncy ball through trap-filled levels using simple one-tap controls. Jump over spikes, avoid lasers, and keep the momentum going as you unlock rewards and strive for the highest score. Perfect for quick sessions with long-term replayability.

Features

  • Arcade Challenge

    Leap from platform to platform in rapid succession.

  • Collect & Unlock

    Gather stars to unlock custom skins and boosters.

  • Trap-Filled Levels

    Navigate lasers, spikes, and crumbling floors.

  • Smooth Controls

    Simple tap-based mechanics for all skill levels.

  • Pulse-Pounding Music

    Energetic tracks to keep the pace alive.

How to Play

Tap once to bounce, time it right to land.

Collect stars while avoiding obstacles.

Use your stars to unlock cool new looks.

Complete daily challenges to boost your score.
